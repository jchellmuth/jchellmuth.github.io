# Ingredients

|Whipping cream|500ml|3oz|  
|Powedered Sugar|50gr||  
|Amarettini|200gr||  
|Sherry|100ml||  

# Notes
* Sandeman Dry Sherry has worked well so far

# Preparation
* Whip Cream with powdered sugar  
* Freeze  
* Break up, stir in sherry and 2/3 of crushed biscuits  
* re-freeze
